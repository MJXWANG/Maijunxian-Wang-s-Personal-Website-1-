Hi! I’m a Cognitive Science student (computational emphasis), currently studying at UC Berkeley. Previously, I was involved in the Grow AI Team, L³Lab @ UC Davis, and the ASPIRE program at UC Davis. My research spans from machine cognition and AI alignment to the societal implications of artificial intelligence.

After transferring to UC Berkeley, I continue to explore cognitive architectures and generative models for AI. I believe AI is a mirror of human intelligence — studying it reflects back on human civilization and wisdom.
