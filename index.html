Hi! I’m a Cognitive Science student (computational emphasis), currently studying at UC Berkeley. Previously, I was involved in the Grow AI initiative, UCD L3 Lab, and the ASPIRE program at UC Davis. My research spans from machine cognition and AI alignment to the societal implications of artificial intelligence.

After transferring to UC Berkeley, I continue to explore cognitive architectures and generative models for AI. My goal is to create general, powerful, trustworthy, and interpretable artificial intelligence. I believe AI is a mirror of human intelligence — studying it reflects back on human civilization and wisdom.
